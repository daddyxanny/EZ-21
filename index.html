About EZ 21 Blackjack Calculator

EZ 21 is a free, professional blackjack strategy calculator that provides real-time probability analysis and optimal play recommendations. Our calculator uses mathematically proven basic strategy to help you make the best decisions at the blackjack table.

Features:

  • Real-time blackjack strategy recommendations
  • Probability calculations for win and bust scenarios
  • Basic strategy based on mathematical analysis
  • Support for multiple card combinations
  • Free to use with no registration required

How to Use:

Simply select your cards and the dealer's up card to receive instant strategy advice. The calculator will tell you whether to hit, stand, or double down based on optimal blackjack basic strategy.
